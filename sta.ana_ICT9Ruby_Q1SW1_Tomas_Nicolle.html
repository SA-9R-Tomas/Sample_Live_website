MUSIC

Music is a kind of art with a combination of vocal or instrumental sounds that expresses our ideas and emotions through the elements of rhythm, harmony, and melody.

WHY I LIKE MUSIC

  • Music makes people happy- Whenever we hear our favorite songs on public we get excited/happy.
  • Music can help us feel emotions- Music is a safe place to feel and where we can explore our emotions. Even sad music can make people express what they feel.
